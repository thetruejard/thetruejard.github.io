Jared Watrous

I'm a 2nd-year Computer Science MS student at Stanford. I received my BA in Computer Science and Minor in Math from UC Berkeley in May 2023. I specialize in classical and machine learning algorithms for computer graphics and vision. I'm especially interested in applications related to film, animation, and other media.

I have been working at Stanford's NeuroAILab since October 2023. My research primarily focuses on novel view synthesis and camera pose prediction using counterfactual world modeling.

I have a strong background in graphics, systems design, and AI. I also have moderate experience with game and web development. On my own time, I like to create short animated films and write music.
