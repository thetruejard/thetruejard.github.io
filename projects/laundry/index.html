"Laundry"

"Laundry" is a WIP 6-minute short film inspired by the post-apocalyptic iyashikei series Girls' Last Tour by author Tsukumizu.

The series features Chito and Yuuri, two girls who are left to fend for themselves as they nagivate the ruins of a long-collapsed civilization. In this short film, Chito stumbles across a clean source of water and takes advantage of the opportunity to do their laundry, only to find an unexpected surprise floating nearby.
